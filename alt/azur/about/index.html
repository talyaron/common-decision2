Delib.org - About:

Goal: Our goal is to help groups reach educates decisions, based on verified knowledge, and the wisdom of all members of the group. In the future we hope to enable cross-groups wisdom. Such decisions should help elevate group-intelligence, help the group reach better decisions, and let every member contribute her unique knowledge, thus, elevating the satisfaction and happiness of most if not all of the members.

Theory: We use a deliberative theory as model for how decsions works. You can find it here.

Utilities:

  1. Business:
    • Help large number of stakeholders, to agree on common goals and solutions.
  2. Politics:
    • Help large audiences produce agenda, goals and solutions.
    • Help communities develop projects together.
  3. Educational:
    • Help students acquire deliberative skills of group decision making.
  4. Imagine
    • In helping build delib.org, the number of utilities is infinite. Delib.org, is a platform for decision making, and decision making is the kernel of our society. Come help us develop and make the world, a better place to live in.
